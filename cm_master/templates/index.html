Welcome to computer hardware engineering course.

The computer hardware engineering diploma course offered at the polytechnic in kerala provides students with a comprehensive education in computer hardware engineering.Through hands-onlearning and access to state-of-the-art labs, students develop a deep umderstanding of computer systems and their components.

  • Curriculum:The course covers processors,memory,storage device,and network systems,as well as design,installation,and maintenance.
  • Hands-on Learning: Students have access to industry-standard software and hardware, allowing them to troubleshoot and repair computer systems in a real-world settings.
  • Experienced faculty: The faculty consists of experienced professionals with industry expertise who provide a supportive and engaging learning environment
  • Career opportunities: Graduates are well prepared for careers in IT,manufacturing,and telicommunications and are in high demand in the workforce.
